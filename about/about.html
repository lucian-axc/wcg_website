Get to know us

- Our mission -

Creating next generation water analysis through on-site spectroscopy and digitalization

- Our vision -

Become a major provider in Scandinavia of efficient, portable analysis systems for water and other liquids

UN Goal 3.9

“By 2030, substantially reduce the number of deaths and illnesses from hazardous chemicals and air, water and soil pollution and contamination.”

UN Goal 6.3

“By 2030, improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping and minimizing release of hazardous chemicals and materials, halving the proportion of untreated wastewater and substantially increasing recycling and safe reuse globally.”

The systems we develop here, at WaterCare Guard, are aimed at helping public institutions and private companies obtain faster results on the water and liquid samples they need to analyze at a fraction of the cost on the market today.

Combined with the ability to detect particles in the range of parts per billion, we enable our customers to perform remediation much sooner.

The final result is that water bodies, their surrounding environment, as well as other affected substances will be cleaner and safer in interaction with human, animal and plant life.
